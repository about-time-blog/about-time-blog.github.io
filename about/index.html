About Time

Hi! I’m Mikkel Riise Lund. I recently turned 1,000,000,000 seconds old. I’m a software developer from Copenhagen, Denmark with a passion for learning and teaching.

I’ve studied at the IT University of Copenhagen, where I specialized in algorithms and data structure - especially related to intervals. I work with cVation, where I bring the global debt market onto the blockchain.

This blog will mostly be about time-related things, like how to use Noda Time to better handle time in .NET, but I will also be using it to document my technical findings and as a playground for trying out new technologies.

I’ve wanted to start a blog for quite some time, but haven’t gotten around doing it. Now it has finally happened, and it’s about time…
